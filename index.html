What It Does

Buyer-safe listing copy

Rewrite the launch promise around source-backed value, clear inputs, clear outputs, price notes, and exclusions.

Claim/evidence matrix

Separate supported, weak, unsupported, and blocked claims before they reach a marketplace, README, or buyer.

First-call risk check

Check schemas, examples, auth, destructive action labels, no-charge errors, idempotency, caps, and buyer instructions.

x402/MCP metadata fixes

Flag missing route/resource binding, query/body discoverability, output contracts, payment metadata, and MCP setup clarity.
